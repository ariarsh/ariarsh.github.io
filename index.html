About me

I recently completed my M.Sc. in Mechatronics Engineering (2022–2025) at the University of Tehran. From 2016–2021, I completed my B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering, also at the University of Tehran. My research centers on control theory, with applications to mechatronic systems and robotics. I’m currently seeking a PhD position in Control Engineering.

Research interests

  • Nonlinear & optimal control (SDRE/ARE, LQR/LQG, MPC)
  • Reinforcement learning for control (actor–critic, DQN, IRL)
  • Robotics & mechatronic systems; modeling and identification
  • Vision-in-the-loop control; real-time experimentation
